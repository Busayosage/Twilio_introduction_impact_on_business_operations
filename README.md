# Twilio Business Impact Analysis – Operational Communications

## Short Overview
This project evaluates how the introduction of Twilio’s communication platform affected credit control operations. By analysing call logs and transactional data using Excel pivot tables, the study assesses changes in call volumes, debtor responses and revenue outcomes after Twilio’s implementation.

## Business Problem
Credit control teams must manage large volumes of customer interactions while ensuring timely payment and effective communication. Prior to adopting Twilio, manual calling processes were inefficient, time‑consuming and produced inconsistent results. The business needed to understand whether a communications platform could streamline operations and improve outcomes.

## Solution / Project Purpose
The project analyses pre‑ and post‑implementation data to measure Twilio’s impact on operations. It aggregates call outcomes, response rates, revenues and timelines to quantify improvements. The analysis also anonymises sensitive data to meet privacy requirements.

## Key Features
- Comparative analysis of call volumes and response rates before and after Twilio adoption.
- Calculation of revenue impact and recovery rates.
- Measurement of time savings in collection processes.
- Data anonymisation to protect customer confidentiality.

## Tools and Technologies
- Microsoft Excel with pivot tables and charts.
- Twilio communications platform data.
- Spreadsheet for data manipulation.

## Workflow / Method
1. Extracted and anonymised credit control call and transaction data.
2. Built pivot tables to summarise call outcomes, durations and revenues.
3. Compared metrics across pre‑Twilio and post‑Twilio periods.
4. Visualised trends and calculated percentage changes.
5. Interpreted results to identify operational improvements.

## Key Insights or Outcomes
The analysis showed that Twilio reduced the number of manual calls required to engage customers and increased the proportion of successful resolutions. Revenue recovery improved and staff spent less time on routine calls, allowing them to focus on more complex cases.

## Business or Practical Impact
By quantifying the benefits of Twilio, the project demonstrates how communication automation can enhance efficiency in credit control operations. These insights support data‑driven decisions on resource allocation and highlight the value of adopting modern communication tools.
